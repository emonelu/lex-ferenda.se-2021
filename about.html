About Us

The association is religiously and politically independent and aims at promoting sound implementation of jurisprudential tenets in both legislation and interpretation of the Law, both in Sweden and internationally.

In addition, Lex Ferenda has the objective of promoting research and development of a knowledge base in the areas of legal doctrine and legal philosophy and disseminate related information to the wider public by means of suitable handouts, implementation of projects and arrangement of events.

The association purports to ask for financial support from Swedish and international donors to perform activities that are conducive to the achievement of these objectives.
